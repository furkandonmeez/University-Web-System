About the University Established with the Law No. 7141 published in the Official Gazette dated May 18, 2018 and numbered 30425, Eskişehir Technical University consists of 5 Faculties, 2 Vocational Schools, 1 School and 3 Institutes and 4 Research and Application Centers in 3 campuses located in the center of Eskişehir. The operation of the international Hasan Polatkan Airport, located in the 2 Eylül Campus of our University, is carried out by our Faculty of Aviation and Space Sciences. There are international flights to Hasan Polatkan Airport operated by different airline companies on various days of the week.
